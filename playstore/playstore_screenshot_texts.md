## Full description draft
Rocket Rivals is a fast-paced local arena combat game for Android. Battle nearby friends or train against AI pilots in short chaotic campaigns. Leave dangerous trails, fire weapons, collect power-ups, dodge traps and survive across classic and user-created arenas.

Features:
- Local multiplayer hosting and joining
- Single-player campaign with AI opponents
- Classic and user-created arenas
- Level editor with blocks, steel, cannons, lasers, gravity fields and teleport doors
- Power-ups, weapons and fast arcade-style rounds
- No account required

Rocket Rivals is currently a pre-release beta build.
