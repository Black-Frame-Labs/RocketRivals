# Privacy Policy - Rocket Rivals Last updated: 2026-01-01 Rocket Rivals is a local multiplayer arcade game developed by BlackFrameLabs. We respect your privacy and aim to collect as little information as possible. ## Information We Collect Rocket Rivals does not require user accounts and does not intentionally collect personal information such as: * Real names * Email addresses * Phone numbers * Contacts * Photos * Messages * Precise location data Player names shown in-game are randomly generated locally on the device. ## Local Multiplayer Communication Rocket Rivals uses nearby device communication technologies such as Bluetooth and local peer-to-peer networking for multiplayer gameplay. This communication is used only for gameplay synchronization between nearby devices. We do not operate online game servers. ## Analytics and Crash Reporting Rocket Rivals uses Google Firebase services including: * Firebase Analytics * Firebase Crashlytics These services may collect anonymous technical information such as: * App launches * Gameplay events * Device type * Operating system version * Crash logs * Performance information This information helps us improve game stability and gameplay quality. We do not use analytics for advertising personalization. We do not sell user data. For more information about Firebase privacy practices, visit: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy ## Version Checking Rocket Rivals may periodically download a small version information JSON file from the official GitHub Pages website in order to: * Check the latest available version * Inform users about updates * Display release information This request does not include personal information. ## Children’s Privacy Rocket Rivals is designed to avoid collecting personal information from children. If you believe personal information has been unintentionally collected, please contact us and we will remove it if possible. ## Third-Party Services Rocket Rivals may use the following third-party services: * Google Firebase Analytics * Google Firebase Crashlytics * GitHub Pages These services may process technical data according to their own privacy policies. ## Data Security We aim to minimize data collection and use reasonable technical measures to protect the limited technical data processed by the application. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. ## Your Rights Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data under laws such as GDPR. Because Rocket Rivals intentionally avoids collecting personally identifiable information, our ability to identify or associate technical data with a specific individual may be limited. ## Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
